## Overview

This extension is designed to help users improve focus and manage browser workflows through Pomodoro-based focus sessions, tab organization, and productivity tracking.
Your privacy matters. This extension does not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

## Information We Collect

### This extension may locally store the following information on your device:

Focus session settings
Custom tab lists/groups
Pomodoro timer preferences
Productivity heatmap/activity data
Extension customization settings

All data is stored locally using Chrome’s extension storage APIs.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

### This extension does not:

Collect personal information
Track browsing history externally
Record keystrokes
Read passwords or sensitive form data
Sell user data
Use third-party analytics or trackers
Transmit your data to external servers

Your browsing activity and productivity data remain on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or sync it using your browser account features.

## Permissions Usage

### The extension may request browser permissions only for functionality directly related to:

Managing tabs during focus sessions
Switching between selected tabs
Saving user preferences and session data
Displaying notifications for timers and breaks

Permissions are used strictly for core extension features.

## Data Storage

All extension-related data is stored locally on your device through Chrome storage mechanisms.
No remote databases or cloud servers are used by this extension.
Third-Party Services
This extension does not integrate with third-party advertising, analytics, or data collection services.
